Quick Tips for Scientific English Communication

1

Use the "Problem-Solution" Structure

Start presentations by clearly stating the problem, then explain your solution. This structure is universally understood in scientific communication.

2

Master Transition Phrases

Learn phrases like "Building on this finding..." or "To investigate this further..." to smoothly connect ideas.

3

Simplify Complex Terms

After introducing technical terms, add a brief explanation in parentheses or with "that is..." to aid understanding.

4

Practice Active Listening

Watch TED Talks or scientific podcasts and repeat phrases you hear to improve pronunciation and rhythm.
